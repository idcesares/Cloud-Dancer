# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and considerate.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, trolling, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing others' private information.
- Personal or political attacks.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
